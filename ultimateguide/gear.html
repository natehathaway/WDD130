Gear Up: What You Need

First and Foremost, you are going to need a disc. It is most often called a frisbee, but that is just one brand of disc to get. The official weight is 175 grams for the Ultimate Disc League (UDL). My personal favorite brand is Discraft Ultrastar. That will always be a super reliable disc, plus they look really cool. Other brands are Frisbee, Pulsar, or Whamo. The 175 gram disc will give you the best ultimate experience!

For shoes, if you are outdoors, I would just recommend soccer cleats for grass, as it gets you great purchase on the ground.

If you are indoors or on a court, either good running shoes or turf shoes work better. It really comes down to what you are comfortable running in.

It is good practice to have some plastic cones to mark touchdown zones, but really all you need is 4 objects to mark the corners of the field and the entrance to the endzones. Just make sure everyone knows the boundaries before you start.
